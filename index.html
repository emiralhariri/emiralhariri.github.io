About Me

I’m a media creator passionate about filmmaking and visual media. Currently studying Audio and Video Media Production at Howard Community College, I’m transferring to Towson University for a degree in Electronic Media and Film.

I’ve worked on commercial, news, and independent projects as a production assistant, camera assistant, and freelance filmmaker. Growing up across Norway, Saudi Arabia, England, and Bahrain shaped my global perspective, and I use film to connect with people and explore different stories.

I honed my craft during a gap year at a Folkehøgskole in Norway, focusing on hands-on media production and traveling for industry insights.
