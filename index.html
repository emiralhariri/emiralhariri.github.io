About

Background and approach

I am a filmmaker and storyteller studying for a BA in Electronic Media and Film at Towson University. I grew up between Norway, Saudi Arabia, England, Bahrain, and now Maryland, which shaped how I see people and place on screen.

My experience spans commercial sets, journalism, and freelance work. I am comfortable across the full pipeline from pre production planning and camera work to editing and sound. A gap year at a Norwegian folkehøgskole helped me lock in my love for visual storytelling through travel and collaboration.

Outside of production I focus on mindfulness and fitness, and I try to bring a calm and present energy to set even when things move fast.
