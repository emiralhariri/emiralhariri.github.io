About Me

I'm a filmmaker and storyteller currently completing my degree at Towson University for a BA in Electronic Media and Film. I was born in Norway and raised in Saudi Arabia, England, and Bahrain before settling in Maryland, that global journey shaped my passion for connecting with people through visual storytelling.

My experience spans commercial sets, journalism, and freelance productions, with a strong foundation in all stages of production—from pre to post. During a gap year at a Norwegian folkehøgskole, I deepened my love for filmmaking through travel, hands-on learning, and creative collaboration.

Outside of film, I find joy in mindfulness, fitness, and crafting, always striving to balance ambition with presence.
